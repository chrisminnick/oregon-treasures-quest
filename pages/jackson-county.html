See a Play at the Oregon Shakespeare Festival

Shall I compare thee, Oregon Shakespeare Festival, to a summer's day? Dazzling audiences since 1935, this magical experience in Jackson County has proven 'tis more lovely and more temperate than just about any festival around as it draws actors from across the globe to perform the iconic works of William Shakespeare on one of the festival's several stages. So, if you're asking yourself "to go or not to go" to a play at the Oregon Shakespeare Festival, know this is unquestionably the best opportunity to see The Bard's canon brought to life on this side of the pond.
